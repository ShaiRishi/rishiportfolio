Driver Drowsiness Detection system

A real-time AI-based system that detects driver fatigue using Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) and facial landmark tracking, developed using Python, OpenCV, dlib, and NumPy. The system continuously monitors eye blink rate and eye closure duration to identify early signs of drowsiness. When fatigue is detected, it instantly triggers an alert to help prevent potential road accidents, ensuring safer driving conditions through continuous monitoring and intelligent vision-based analysis.
